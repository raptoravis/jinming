We look after your pets, while you take a pause

We are a small, friendly home petcare service based in St Albans.

Whether you're going on holiday or just working long hours, we can visit your home to ensure your pet is cared for and exercised while you are away.

Why home visits?

Having a home pet carer prevents the stress and medical risks of placing your pet in a kennels or cattery. Staying at home while you are away ensures that your pet keeps its usual routine in familiar surroundings.

Over and above

As part of our standard service, we will provide you with regular updates from your pet - via your choice of text or email - so that you can relax in the knowledge that your pet is happy and well cared for in your absence.

Should your pet need regular medication and/or visits to the vet we are fully insured to do this for you. We believe that maintaining a routine is particularly important for animals that are elderly or in ill health.

And it’s not just cats and dogs. We understand the additional care and attention that puppies and kittens need while you are away and happy to create a care package thats caters for this. We can also look after any small caged pets or fishtanks in your absence.

We are happy to accommodate any special requirements your pet may have, however big or small!
