Overview

Jinming International Limited was founded in 2006, it has more than 30 qualified engineers and managers, and company has a variety of corporate divisions in domestic and overseas subsidiary, including design, engineering, manufacturing and international Trade.

Main scope of business

  • Building materials, fire protection products import and export sales
  • Mechanical and electrical engineering design, construction
  • Production and surface treatment for metal products
  • Import and export daily supplies

Throughout 10 years of solidarity, cooperation and hard work, we obtained a remarkable achievement today, not only successfully undertook the gaming industry, offered building material and entire construction services as well as government projects.

Projects include

Venetian Resort & Hotel Macau, Sand Resort Macau ,Sands center Macau, Sheraton hotel, Conrad hotel, ST hotel, Holiday Inn hotel, Star World Casino & hotel ,Galaxy Resort & hotel, Broadway Casino & hotel, L'Arc Casino & hotel, New YoHan Shopping Mall,DSAT, the High Court, Macau University, High School of Haojiang, CEM , border building of QingZhou, parking of IouHan, parking of Maliujia. This stimulates company’s achievement and rapid development.


In order to repay a trust of our customers, the company is going to expand its professional excellence, dedicate to a continuous development, improve the quality of professional service. We are willing to commit with all excellent people together, to create more professional and precision products in order to reach to a new high.
