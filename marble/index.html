Dogs

We aim to tailor our home pet care package to your specific needs, so that you only pay for what you are getting. Even if you have more than one dog, we will only charge per visit or walk.

Dog walking

We offer dog walking for £12 per hour (minimum one hour), with the option to increase the length of the walk for £6 per 30 mins. We will drive to your home and walk your dog in their familiar surroundings.

Home visits

Basic package £10 per visit. This can include:

  • Feeding / watering
  • Cleaning up
  • Short walk
  • Play and cuddle
  • Regular updates from your dog via text or email
  • Basic housesitting: water plants, pick up post, put out bins, switch lights on/off as required

We understand that different dogs have different exercise requirements. Additional dog walking can be added at £6 per 30 mins.

We also offer house sitting, subject to availability and quoted on a case by case basis. Please contact us for further details.

Contact us

Please contact us to discuss your specific requirements, or to arrange a free home visit to meet you and your dog!
